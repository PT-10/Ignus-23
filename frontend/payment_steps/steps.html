How to Pay?

1) Go to https://www.onlinesbi.sbi/sbicollect/ and read the terms and condition and click the checkbox to proceed.
2) Select Rajasthan as the state of institutition and the type of institution to Educational Institution.
Click "Go" to proceed further.
3) Search and select IGNUS in the educational institutions and click Submit.
4) Select Registration and Entry Fee in Payment category.
5) Enter the required details as instructed and click submit to proceed to the payment.
6) Close the tab after the payment!

* The details of your transaction and passes will be updated on the website every 8 hours, so don't panic if you can't see the pass on your profile right after the payment, come back later and you can see it on your profile page *
