About Us

IGNUS, the much anticipated annual socio-cultural fest of IIT Jodhpur, is now back, after three years. Ignus celebrates art, culture and pluralism. It provides a stage for the creative and intellectual minds from across the country to showcase their incredible talent and creativity through art and culture.

Bohemian Convoy

Our theme for this year is Bohemian Convoy : a socially unconventional journey.
Bohemians are wanderers and vagabonds and don't we all want little adventures in our life to keep us going? IGNUS has been celebrating art and culture for years now but this year IGNUS is celebrating the uniqueness of every individual expressed through art and culture. So, just like Bohemians, let's be carefree for these 4 days and celebrate our eccentricities together.
